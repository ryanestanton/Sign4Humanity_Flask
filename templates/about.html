ABOUT US

Over 5% of the world suffers from some degree of hearing loss. That's over 360 million people...

With Sign4Humanity, we seek to promote more Sign Language in the world by enabling users to practice communication skills.

Just like popular apps like iMessages, users can "text" using our program and formulate sentences through a series of signs.

Why not just text?

In a world where 5% of it's population suffers from a degree of hearing loss, you could potentially encounter someone face to face who relies on sign language as a method of communication.

Therefore, we cannot just pull out a device and "text."

Finally, we seek to be inclusive and make everyone feel respected.

Sign Language is how we can do that.
